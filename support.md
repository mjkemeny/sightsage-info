# SightSage Support

For questions, feedback, or bug reports, please email us at sightsageinfo@gmail.com.

We typically respond within 72 hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?**
Open SightSage, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your Pro access 
will be restored automatically.

**How do I change which exercise modes are active?**
Tap the icon on the exercise screen to open the library. Use the mode grid 
to enable or disable any stimulus-response combination, then click Start.
