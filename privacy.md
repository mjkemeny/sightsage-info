# SightSage Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SightSage stores all user data locally on your device. The app does not collect, 
transmit, or share any personal information.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used.

Your practice progress, settings, and preferences remain entirely on your device 
and are never sent to any server.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at sightsageinfo@gmail.com.
